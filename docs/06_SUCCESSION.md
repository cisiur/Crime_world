# Succession System

## Core idea

The organization should survive beyond the player character when possible.

## Possible succession outcomes

- stable transfer to a chosen heir
- transfer to a child or family member
- transfer to a trusted lieutenant
- contested succession
- civil war inside the organization
- fragmentation into smaller groups
- hostile takeover by an outsider

## Player death

If the boss dies and no heir is defined, the organization should enter crisis.

## Player imprisonment

If the boss is imprisoned, the designated successor can temporarily run the organization.
The imprisoned boss may still affect decisions depending on communication, corruption, and organization stability.

## Internal tension

A successor may attempt to seize power permanently.
This can create loyalty problems, betrayals, and internal war.

## Generational gameplay

Successors should make the campaign feel dynastic rather than strictly character-based.
The player is building a legacy, not just a save file.
