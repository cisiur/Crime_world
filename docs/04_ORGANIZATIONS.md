# Organizations

## Purpose

Organizations are the main units of power in the game. The player controls one organization, but many others exist in the world and compete for influence.

## Types of organizations

- street gangs
- family mafias
- cartels
- corruption networks
- mercenary crews
- business fronts
- political machines
- hybrid syndicates

## Organization properties

Each organization should have:
- leadership structure
- ideology or culture
- specialization
- territory
- allies
- rivals
- resources
- reputation
- fear factor
- loyalty level
- internal stability

## Internal hierarchy

A typical criminal organization can include:
- boss
- underboss
- lieutenants
- capos
- crews
- associates
- street-level workers

## Organization behavior

Organizations should act independently and pursue their own goals:
- expand territory,
- defend assets,
- form alliances,
- retaliate against attacks,
- recruit characters,
- pressure rivals,
- influence politics,
- protect or betray leadership.

## Conflict

Conflict is not only combat.
It also includes:
- market pressure,
- bribery,
- corruption,
- sabotage,
- intimidation,
- propaganda,
- civil war inside the organization,
- takeover attempts.
