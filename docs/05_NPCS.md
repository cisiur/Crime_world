# NPC System

## Philosophy

Important characters should feel like living actors with motives, relationships, and long-term ambitions.

## Character attributes

Important NPCs can have:
- age
- health
- personality
- loyalty
- ambition
- intelligence
- brutality
- greed
- family ties
- secrets
- reputation
- wealth
- trauma
- addictions
- skills
- goals

## Roles

NPCs can be:
- gang members
- lieutenants
- business owners
- police officers
- politicians
- criminals
- heirs
- bodyguards
- informants
- rivals

## Behavior

NPCs should make decisions based on:
- self-preservation,
- profit,
- fear,
- loyalty,
- ambition,
- family,
- organization interests,
- current world conditions.

## Important NPCs

Not every NPC needs full simulation.
Only meaningful characters should have deep behavior and long-term memory.

## Relationships

Relationships matter between characters, families, organizations, and factions.
Useful relationship values include:
- trust
- respect
- fear
- hatred
- dependency
- debt
- kinship
- betrayal risk
