# Gameplay Loop

## Start phase

1. Create the player character.
2. Choose a real city.
3. Pick a first hideout or safe location.
4. Begin with almost no resources.

## Early game

The player survives through small operations such as:
- theft,
- intimidation,
- minor contracts,
- stolen goods,
- street-level recruitment,
- low-risk illegal work.

## Mid game

The player starts to build a real organization through:
- recruitment,
- territory control,
- safehouses,
- legal front businesses,
- logistics,
- corruption,
- alliances,
- sabotage of rivals.

## Late game

The player expands into:
- multiple districts,
- multiple cities,
- cross-city supply chains,
- political influence,
- regional domination,
- dynastic succession.

## Player agency

The player chooses long-term goals such as:
- becoming the largest organization,
- monopolizing a sector,
- dominating a city,
- building a global syndicate,
- influencing government,
- controlling the underworld.

## Fail states

The campaign can fail through:
- death with no heir,
- organizational collapse,
- hostile takeover,
- total bankruptcy,
- complete loss of influence.
