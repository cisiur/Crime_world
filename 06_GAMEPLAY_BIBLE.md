# Gameplay Bible

## Gameplay Loop

1.  Create a character.
2.  Select a city.
3.  Choose a safehouse.
4.  Earn initial capital.
5.  Recruit people.
6.  Expand operations.
7.  Build legal businesses.
8.  Control districts.
9.  Expand to other cities.
10. Build a global syndicate.

The player plans operations while NPCs execute them.
