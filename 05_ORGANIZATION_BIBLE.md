# Organization Bible

## Hierarchy

Boss - Underboss - Capos - Lieutenants - Crews - Associates

## Characters

Important NPCs have:

-   Personality
-   Loyalty
-   Ambition
-   Family
-   Wealth
-   Secrets
-   Relationships
-   Goals

## Succession

Organizations continue after the Boss dies.

Possible outcomes: - Stable succession - Civil war - Split
organization - External takeover
