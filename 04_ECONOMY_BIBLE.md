# Economy Bible

## Economic Sectors

-   Transport
-   Construction
-   Retail
-   Hospitality
-   Real Estate
-   Media
-   Agriculture
-   Industry
-   Gambling
-   Drug Trade

Each business contributes to:

-   Income
-   Influence
-   Logistics
-   Cover
-   Operations

The economy is simulated through sector interactions rather than
individual purchases.
