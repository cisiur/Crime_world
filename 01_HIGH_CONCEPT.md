# High Concept

## Elevator Pitch

Build a multi-generational criminal empire in any real city in the world
using procedurally generated gameplay based on OpenStreetMap.

## Core Vision

The player starts as a single unknown person with no organization.
Through strategic planning, recruitment, legal and illegal businesses,
diplomacy and territorial expansion, they create an international
criminal syndicate.

## Genre

-   Grand Strategy
-   Management
-   Sandbox
-   Crime Simulation
-   RPG-lite

## Inspirations

-   Crusader Kings (dynasties & characters)
-   Infection Free Zone (real-world maps)
-   RimWorld (story generation)
